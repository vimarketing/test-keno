What we stand for

Three commitments. No marketing inflation.

Lottery vendors say a lot of things in the first email. Here is what we actually deliver, written so a CPO and a CTO can both check it before signing.

i.

Balkan insider, not Balkan tourist

We hire callers from Belgrade, Zagreb, and Bucharest because audio matters. We track MUP RS, MinFin HR, ONJN agendas because reporting rules change quarterly. We do not subcontract the locale layer.

ii.

Compliance is the product, not the appendix

GLI-19 lab certification, MGA B2B Critical Supply, WLA RG Framework L1 are baseline, not differentiators. The compliance pack ships in week one — math model audit, RG controls, regulator mapping, supplier register entry, RTP attestation, SAR documentation.

iii.

Four weeks, four conditions

Sandbox-to-live in four weeks is real if four upstream conditions are real: a locked lobby slot, a green-lit RGS handshake, a real device matrix, and a named integration engineer on each side. We tell you which of the four are still red on day one.
